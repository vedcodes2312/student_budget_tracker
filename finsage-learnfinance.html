Basic Finance Terminologies

Risk

The possibility of losing money on an investment or not achieving expected returns.

Investment

The act of allocating money to assets like stocks, bonds, or real estate to generate income or profit.

Savings

Money that is set aside for future use instead of spending immediately.

Mutual Funds

Investment vehicles that pool money from many investors to invest in a diversified portfolio.

Policies

Financial agreements like insurance or retirement plans that define protection or benefits.
